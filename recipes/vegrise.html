Vegrise

Description

    First, prep all your veggies by cutting them into thin, long slices. Make sure your rice is cooked and preferably cold (leftover rice works best for fried rice). Heat about 1.5 tablespoons of oil in a wok or large pan on high flame. Add the finely chopped garlic and green chili, and stir it until it starts smelling

    Then toss in the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent. Now, add the rest of the mixed vegetables and stir-fry them on high flame for about 3 to 5 minutes—don’t overcook, we want them crunchy. Next, add soy sauce, vinegar, and pepper powder, and mix everything well. Add the cooked rice and gently mix everything together on high heat—don’t mash the rice. Taste and add salt if needed. Finally, top it off with chopped spring onions if you have some, and boom, your veg fried rice is ready to be devoured!

Ingredients

  1. Cooked rice – 2 cups (preferably cold or leftover rice)
  2. Mixed veggies – 1 cup (carrot, beans, capsicum, peas, cabbage)
  3. Onion – 1 small, chopped
  4. Spring onion – optional, for garnish
  5. Garlic – 4-5 cloves, finely chopped
  6. Green chili – 1 (optional)
  7. Soy sauce – 1.5 tsp
  8. Vinegar – 1/2 tsp
  9. Pepper powder – 1/2 tsp
  10. Salt – as needed
  11. Oil – 1.5 tbsp (use sesame or regular cooking oil)
