Salad

Description

    Allrecipes has curated a list of 16 fall and winter salads that highlight seasonal ingredients like butternut squash, sweet potatoes, Brussels sprouts, apples, and cranberries. These hearty salads are perfect for the colder months and can serve as appetizers or main courses with added proteins

    Notable recipes include the Apple, Pomegranate, and Pecan Salad with homemade vinaigrette; Spinach and Persimmon Salad with Goat Cheese and Pomegranate; and Kale Sweet Potato Salad featuring roasted sweet potatoes

    Other highlights are the Roasted Butternut Squash with Onions, Spinach, and Cranberries; Butternut Squash Caprese Salad; and Roasted Sweet Potato Salad with Feta. For a twist on traditional flavors, there's the Autumn Waldorf Salad using yogurt instead of mayo, and a Shaved Brussels Sprouts Caesar Salad topped with bacon and croutons

Ingredients

  1. Apples (sliced or chopped)
  2. Pomegranate seeds
  3. Fresh kale or spinach
  4. Roasted sweet potatoes or butternut squash
  5. Goat cheese or feta cheese
  6. Pecans or walnuts
  7. Dried cranberries
  8. Red onions (thinly sliced)
  9. Olive oil
  10. Apple cider vinegar or balsamic vinegar
  11. Salt and pepper
  12. Maple syrup or honey (for dressings)
